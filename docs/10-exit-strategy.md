Do not sell under 4× ARR.

## Target Exit
- £750k ARR

## Buyer Types
- Payroll platforms
- FSM tools
- Small PE / rollups

## What Buyers Care About
- Low churn
- Embedded payroll workflow
- Reduced founder dependency
- Clean financials

This is an asset build, not a salary play.
