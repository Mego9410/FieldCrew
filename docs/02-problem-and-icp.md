# Problem & ICP

## Core Problem

Small HVAC businesses lose money silently through:
- Untracked or mis-attributed hours
- Overtime leakage
- No visibility into labour cost per job
- Payroll run without job context

Owners often know revenue per job but **not labour cost per job**.

This makes margin control impossible.
